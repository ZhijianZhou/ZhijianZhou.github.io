Distributional Reinforcement Learning for Large Language Models

Zhijian Zhou, et al.

We propose a distributional actor-critic framework for LLM reasoning that learns the full distribution of returns to guide exploration more effectively. Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on mathematical reasoning benchmarks including AIME, MATH500, and OlympiadBench.
